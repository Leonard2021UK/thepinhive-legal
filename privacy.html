Privacy Policy

The Pin Hive · Effective 22 July 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how The Pin Hive ("we", "us") handles information in connection with our Pinterest presence (@thepinhive) and the automation tools we use to publish our own content. The Pin Hive is a personal content curation project that shares product recommendations on Pinterest as an Amazon Associate.

Who this policy covers

The Pin Hive operates a single Pinterest business account that publishes original pins linking to products on Amazon. We do not run a consumer-facing service, we do not offer accounts or logins to members of the public, and we do not collect personal information from visitors as part of this project.

Information we access

Our software connects to the Pinterest API using OAuth, strictly to manage our own account. Through this connection we access:

  • Our own Pinterest account profile and board list (to route pins to the correct board);
  • Our own pins and their public performance metrics (to understand which content performs well).

We do not access, request, or store personal data belonging to other Pinterest users, their boards, or their private information.

How we use it

Access is used solely to create, schedule, and measure the performance of pins on our own account. We do not sell, rent, or share any data with third parties for advertising or any other purpose.

Data storage and security

API credentials (access and refresh tokens) are stored privately in our own environment and are never published or shared. Performance metrics for our own pins may be stored in a private database used only by us.

Affiliate disclosure

As an Amazon Associate, The Pin Hive earns from qualifying purchases. Some links we publish are affiliate links, meaning we may receive a commission at no additional cost to you if you purchase through them.

Third-party services

Our pins link to Amazon. When you follow a link, the destination site's own privacy policy governs how it handles your information. We encourage you to review Pinterest's and Amazon's privacy policies.

Changes

We may update this policy from time to time. The effective date above reflects the latest version.

Contact
